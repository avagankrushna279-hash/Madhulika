Our Story

Authentic Devgad Hapus — Nothing Less

Nestled in the heart of Thane West, Moreshwar Mango Shop brings you the most prized mangoes from the Konkan coast. Our Devgad Alphonso mangoes are celebrated for their rich aroma, silky texture, and a sweetness so deep it lingers long after the last bite.

Every mango is handpicked from orchards where the trees breathe sea air and drink coastal soil. We preserve them the old way — naturally, with time and care, never with chemicals. What you taste is exactly what the tree gave us.
